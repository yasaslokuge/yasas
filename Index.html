Brief History

Sri Lanka, also known as Ceylon, is a tropical island nation with a long history, located near the southeast tip of the Indian subcontinent.

The Dehiwala National Zoo, which has a remarkable collection of exotic and indigenous fauna, is one of the oldest Zoos in Asia. The Zoo in Sri Lanka is widely known as Colombo Zoo all over the world.

Currently, Dehiwala Zoo is home to 72 mammal species, 65 bird species, 31 reptile species, 89 fish species, and 30 butterfly species, among others. Furthermore they states that there are over 3000 animal species in various breeding and free-living colonies of birds, with a focus on rare and endangered species conservation and breeding.
